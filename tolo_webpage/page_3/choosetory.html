HOW CAN I ASSIST YOU TODAY?

One standout song is "Virtual Insanity," where Taeyong expresses confidence in himself, imagining himself as a character in a virtual world and possessing the same abilities in reality. He showcases his boldness by likening himself to powerful characters like Son Goku from the manga
"Dragon Ball" or Grand Chariot from the manga "Super Granprix," who possess unparalleled strength that others cannot overlook. This selfassuredness is evident in the song as well.

Another track, "404 File Not Found," is about feeling lost in life, like when you can’t find a webpage (you know, the "404 error").
The lyrics talks about looking at yourself in the mirror brand feeling unsure. But Taeyong also encourages listeners not to stay stuck and to find their own path,
even if it's not the perfect answer.

The song "RUBY" tells a story about memories and regrets with a past lover. It's interesting because Taeyong had a dog named "Ruby" before,
so the song goes beyond just romantic relationships. It's about valuing all the things we hold dear. The lyrics as the end suggest a longing for meeting again in the future, not just with romantic partners, but with all the important connections in our lives.

In "Back to the Past," Taeyong talks about his past experiences in a calm way. He reflects on a tough childhood, with adults saying negative things about him and even dealing with a leaking roof during heavy rain. But he also shows that he has succeeded because of his own efforts.
He expresses gratitude towards his family for going through those tough times together. The song shows how those painful experiences
shaped who he is today.

"관둬" ("Let It Go") is about wanting to let go of lingering feelings for a past love. The lyrics talk about regreting not being able to move on, even
when you want to start a new love. But by saying "let it go," the song shows a determination to break free from the past and move forward.

"Move Mood Mode" is a song featuring Red Velvet's Wendy. It captures the excitement of new love, like when you start a fresh relationship. It
describes the joy and sweetness of falling in love, using phrases like "Just by looking at that person, my heart crumbles" and "I love every
aspect of you."
